## What is the result? - Level 1

What is the result of each of the following statements?

1. `"abc"*3`
2. `"abc"*2.5`
3. `"abc"*2.0`
4. `"abc"*True`
5. `"abc"*False`
6. `print("xy\tz")`
7. `print("xy\"z\'")`
8. `"qwer" "xyz"`

### Solution

1. `abcabcabc`
2. `TypeError`
3. `TypeError`
4. `"abc"`
5. `""`
6. `xy	z`
7. `xy"z'`
8. `'qwerxyz'`
